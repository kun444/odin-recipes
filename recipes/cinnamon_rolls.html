Cinnamon rolls

Description

These bakery style Homemade Cinnamon Rolls are big, fluffy, and super soft! The dough is sweet, rises perfectly every time and bakes up nicely. The filling is made with softened butter, brown sugar, cinnamon and two different flavor extracts (itâ€™s my secret ingredient!). To top it off, the cinnamon roll frosting is thick, sweet, and delicious! These are the best cinnamon rolls you will ever have!

Ingredients

  • 100g milk
  • Cinnamon
  • 7g dry yeast

    Steps

  1. Activate the yeast.
  2. Add wet ingredients, then dry ingredients.
  3. Make the filling.
  4. Roll out the cinnamon roll dough.
  5. Add the butter and cinnamon roll filling.
  6. Bake.
